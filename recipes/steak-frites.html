Steak Frites


Description:

A classic French bistro dish featuring a juicy pan-seared or grilled steak served alongside crispy golden fries. Simple yet elegant, it’s all about quality ingredients and bold flavor.


Ingredients:

  • 2 ribeye or sirloin steaks
  • Salt and pepper
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 cloves garlic (smashed)
  • Fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)
  • 2 large russet potatoes
  • Oil for frying (vegetable or canola)


Steps:

  1. Prep the fries: Peel and cut potatoes into thin sticks. Soak in cold water for 30 minutes, then dry thoroughly
  2. Cook the fries: Heat oil to 350°F (175°C). Fry potatoes in batches for 4–5 minutes until golden. Drain and season with salt.
  3. Cook the steak: Season steaks with salt and pepper. Sear in a hot skillet with butter, garlic, and herbs for 2–3 minutes per side (medium-rare), basting with butter.
  4. Rest: Let the steak rest for 5 minutes before slicing.
  5. Serve: Plate with fries and enjoy with mustard or herb butter.
