Lasagna


Description:

hearty classic Italian dish layered with rich meat sauce, creamy bechamel,

and melted cheese â€” perfect for family dinners or meal prep.


Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 and 1/2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Optional: Italian seasoning or basil


Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375 Degrees
  2. Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain and set to the side
  3. Cook beef in a skillet until browned. Add marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes
  4. In a bowl, combine ricotta, egg, Parmesan, salt, pepper, and italian seasoning
  5. Assemble in a 9x13" baking dish:
    • Spread a little meat sauce on the bottom
    • Add 3 noodles
    • Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture
    • Add meat sauce
    • Sprinkle mozzarella
    • Repeat these steps 3 times to build layers
  6. Top layer: Finish with sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan
  7. Bake covered with foil for 25 minutes. Uncover and bake for 10 more minutes until browned
  8. Let rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving
